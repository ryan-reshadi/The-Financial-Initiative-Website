Our Story

From Bracelets to
Lasting Change.

We started as Loop Life — a small initiative selling handcrafted bracelets to spark conversations about money and mindset. Every purchase supported the Young Investors Society and helped fund youth financial education.

Today, we've grown into The Financial Initiative — a full nonprofit dedicated to delivering free, hands-on financial literacy workshops to students and communities, including international programs run in partnership with Green Silicon Valley.

Community-First Education
We bring financial knowledge to the communities that need it most, for free.
International Reach
Running workshops across borders through our partnership with Green Silicon Valley.
Youth-Led & Mission-Driven
Founded and operated by students who believe knowledge is the first step to freedom.
What We Offer

Programs Built
to Empower

From beginner budgeting to investment fundamentals — our free workshops meet students where they are.

01
Budgeting & Saving Fundamentals
Hands-on workshops that make personal budgeting approachable. Students build their first real budget, learn the 50/30/20 rule, and discover practical saving strategies they can use immediately.
50/30/20 Rule Emergency Funds Goal Setting
Join this workshop
02
Investing for Beginners
Demystifying the stock market, index funds, and compound interest for young learners. Run in partnership with the Young Investors Society, this workshop turns intimidating concepts into clear, actionable steps.
Stock Market Basics Index Funds Compound Interest
Join this workshop
03
International Workshops
In partnership with Green Silicon Valley, we host financial literacy workshops internationally — bringing money mindset skills and confidence to students around the world through live and virtual sessions.
Virtual Sessions GSV Partnership 4 Countries
Join this workshop
04
Debt & Credit Literacy
Understanding credit scores, managing debt responsibly, and navigating student loans are skills too few young people are taught. This workshop fills that gap with real-world scenarios and clear frameworks.
Credit Scores Student Loans Debt Payoff
Join this workshop
05
ESG & Sustainable Investing
An introduction to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) investing — how values-aligned portfolios work, why companies are rated on sustainability, and how young investors can build a future they believe in.
ESG Ratings Green Finance Impact Investing
